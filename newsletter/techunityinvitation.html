Tech Unity - Formal Invitation

Esteemed members of the Baltimore Metropolitan Economy

Happy hump day. It has been an honor to meet many of you in person. For everyone else, I hope to meet you at Tech Unity! In putting on the Tech Unity event, Code Collective is furthering the work of Baltimore's economic development. This event is on June 28th, 2025, at 1:00PM. We will be serving tacos and encouraging economic collaboration.

Special consideration has been given to our Tech Affinity groups, which are already gathering the people together in virtuous assembly, and encouraging them in the things they love to do. These well-run organizations are essential points of conversation between the economy and the people.

Furthermore, we recognize the Non-Profits in the Baltimore area, who are already engaged in economic work I can only dream of.

Finally, we would like to reach out to any group with manufacturing capability, to recognize the value of goods produced, and to align ourselves to support their productivity.

All of these groups will be given time to speak during the ceremony, for which I've allotted an hour. Representatives of other groups may contact me directly.

Here's to a mighty local economy!
