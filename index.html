About Us

A Call to Virtue

Technology is essential in the current economy. Collective exists to empower virtuous technologists to create a better economy.

The Voice of Baltimore's Best Technologists

Code Collective empowers local technologists through educational workshops, community-driven meetups, and advocacy for change in the economy. By reforming labor and incentive systems, we strengthen the institutions around us. Our approach combines necessary skills teaching with collective decision making, thereby creating an economy that works for the people.

Empowered Tech Labor

The hiring process of today is broken. Companies spend significant resources finding employees, but still seem to hire the wrong people. Code Collective aims to improve the hiring pipeline by evaluating candidates in real development environments before recommending them to employers. With a deeper understanding of the current tech landscape, we offer a practical approach to hiring where others have failed.

Influential Projects

At Code Collective, we tackle a diverse range of projects that bridge technology and real-world impact. There are two major projects underway; the Arkavo Secure Communications platform and Baltimore ASI (Artificial Super Intelligence). These projects are both a means to develop local technologists and of monumental importance in themselves.


Code Collective is a cybernetic syncretism emphasizing professionalism in Computer Science. The org is currently guided by the unofficial Constitution and Bylaws
