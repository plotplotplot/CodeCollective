What we’re about

We're creating a start-up ecosystem in Greater Washington area. Our mission is to provide resources and networking opportunities to entrepreneurs and innovators. Join us in building a stronger, more vibrant start-up community in our area. Connect with us to learn more.

  • Tue, Jul 8, 2025, 6:00 PM EDTTechnical Readiness Level for Founders
    Northeastern University Rosslyn Campus, Arlington, VA

    RSVPs are required due to venue safety precautions. Anyone that's not on the RSVP list by July 7th 7pm will not be able to attend the event.

    Agenda:
    6pm: Networking session
    7pm: Speaker begins and Q&A afterwards

    Are you a founder or in the startup scene, and wondering if you're ready to go in front of investors? In this event, seasoned startup founder and investor Jonathan Jenkins will be sharing his experience from both perspectives and answer any questions you may have.

    Jonathan Jenkins is a Marine Corps veteran turned seasoned business and technology leader, with over 30 years of experience as an executive, inventor, coach, and public speaker focused on helping organizations enhance their leadership and innovation. He currently serves as Strategic Growth Advisor, mentor to many medium to large scale companies, and as well as a VC advisor.

    During this event, Jonathan will be pulling back the curtain on how investment process works and share applicable tips with startup founders. If you're interested in learning more about the investment process, join us on July 8th in Northeastern University's Rosslyn location.
