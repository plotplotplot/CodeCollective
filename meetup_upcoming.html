What we’re about

This group is focused on bringing together the Maryland Red Hat User Community for technical presentations, conversations, good food and drinks in a very laid-back setting. Membership includes Red Hat Customers, Partners, Employees, and Enthusiasts.

Take charge of your inbox and get the latest product updates, information on trials, promotions, events, and more, directly from Red Hat by selecting areas of interest (and update your preferences anytime): https://engage.redhat.com/Global-Preference-Center?
