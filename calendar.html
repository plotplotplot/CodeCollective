2025-05-04 13:00:00 EST

@Reveille Grounds

Industrial 3D Modeling with OpenSCAD [Free]

Come learn the basics of 3D modeling with OpenSCAD — a powerful, free design tool where you create models using simple code! In this beginner-friendly workshop, we’ll guide you step-by-step as you write your name in 3D using OpenSCAD, preparing it for 3D printing.

Bring your Windows or Linux laptop!

We’ll help you get set up, write your first OpenSCAD script, and customize your design. By the end of the session, you’ll have a 3D model of your name, ready to print and take home a piece of your creativity! No prior coding or 3D design experience needed — just bring yourself, your laptop, and your curiosity.

🛠️ What You'll Learn:

  • Installing and running OpenSCAD (Windows & Linux)
  • Writing simple OpenSCAD code
  • Making 3D text models
  • Saving your work for 3D printing

Version Control Certification [Free]

Version Control Workshop: Essential Skills for Modern Development Join us for an engaging 45-minute workshop on GitHub, the industry-standard platform for code collaboration and version control. Whether you're a beginner or looking to enhance your existing skills, this hands-on session as part of STEM Rebellion at Reveille Grounds in Baltimore will get you up to speed with professional workflows.

This workshop offers an excellent opportunity to expand your professional network while learning skills that are crucial in today's development landscape. Many employers consider GitHub proficiency a must-have skill, making this workshop valuable for career growth.

What to bring:

  • Your laptop
  • Your mobile phone (for 2FA)

What we'll provide:

  • One-on-one support from experienced developers
  • A personalized Version Control Basic Proficiency certification to any who complete the exercise

Code Collective is a leading professional development organization dedicated to advancing technical skills in the community.

Don't miss this chance to boost your development toolkit, earn a credential, and connect with fellow professionals in the Baltimore tech community!

Tech Unity

@Dear Globe Coffee Roasters

Run Your Own Social Media [IN-PERSON]

React

Join us at Code Collective to master modern web development and launch your own social media instance: the arkavo open-source devsecops inspired site. You'll get hands-on experience with professional tools and technologies. This workshop will be focused on React development.

If you would like to run your own network, you will need a cloud VM (ex. EC2) with 24GB of storage, preferably running Ubuntu. Otherwise, you can run a local React app targeting our sandbox network.

Bring a laptop! You can run a basically functional site locally, right on your laptop. If you have an AWS account, we can help you set up your own social media live!
