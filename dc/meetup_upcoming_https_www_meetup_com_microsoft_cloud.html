About us

Welcome to the Microsoft Cloud + AI Meetup! This group is for individuals who are passionate about Microsoft's Cloud and Artificial Intelligence technologies including Azure, Microsoft 365, and Copilot. Whether you are a beginner looking to learn the basics or an experienced professional wanting to deepen your knowledge, this Meetup is for you. Join us for regular networking events, workshops, and presentations from industry experts and directly from Microsoft Subject Matter Experts. Let's come together to share our insights, experiences, and best practices when it comes to utilizing the power of the Microsoft Cloud and AI!
