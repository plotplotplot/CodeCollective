What we’re about

This is a group for IT and other professionals in the Washington and beyond, who intend to advance and share their understanding on IT domains like Cloud, Networking, Security, Data, AI, Development, Project Management, Leadership, Design & Multimedia. All skill levels are welcome to join.

We mostly promote online and live events related to IT, soft skills and business productivity for free and keep you up-to-date about the latest industry trends. These meetups bring you a great opportunity to engage with peers and enhance your knowledge and skillset. Our wide range of events focus to provide insights on highly-demanded technologies like Microsoft, Cisco, Adobe, AutoCAD, VMware, Adobe and many more.

We aim to promote the values of lifelong learning by interconnecting likeminded tech lovers and sharing diversified skills and knowledge on a single platform
