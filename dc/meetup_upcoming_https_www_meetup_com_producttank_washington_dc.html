About us

The DC Tech Product Management Meetup is for Product Managers to share and gain knowledge from each other. This is a meetup for Product People by Product People to help foster a true community of us in the greater DC area. We're a small community, with a very challenging (and often differing), role and we can all help other create amazing products.

This meetup is for you if:

• You're in charge of what gets built by your product team.

• You're in charge of how your product goes to market.

• You're in charge of learning what to do next.
