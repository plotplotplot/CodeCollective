About us

The DC Chapter of the Sierra Club is a volunteer-led, people-powered chapter of the Sierra Club that actively works to tackle the climate crisis, ensure clean air and water, and protect nearby nature and the environment in the District of Columbia.

Join us as we protect, explore and enjoy the environment in our nation's capital!

See our updated Calendar & RSVP here: www.dc.sierraclub.org/calendar
Our website is: www.sierraclub.org/dc
