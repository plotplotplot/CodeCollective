About us

# 🤖 Data & AI Meetup: Connect. Learn. Innovate.

The world of data and AI is moving fast—don’t navigate it alone! Join the Analytics.CLUB Meetup, where professionals, learners, and leaders come together to explore the latest in data science, machine learning, analytics, and artificial intelligence.
Whether you’re a beginner curious about AI, a data professional looking to sharpen your skills, or an industry leader shaping the future, this is the place to connect, share, and grow.

### What to Expect:

  • 🔹 Expert Talks & Panels – Hear from leaders in data, analytics, and AI.
  • 🔹 Hands-on Learning – Practical insights, tools, and strategies you can apply right away.
  • 🔹 Networking Opportunities – Meet peers, mentors, and collaborators who share your passion.
  • 🔹 Career & Growth Tips – Learn about jobs, projects, and skills that will keep you ahead of the curve.

### About Analytics.CLUB

Founded in 2013, Analytics.CLUB is the world’s largest volunteer-led analytics and AI community—built by data people, for data people. From Fortune 100 executives to emerging talent, our members come together online and offline to share knowledge, build careers, and advance the field of data and AI.
📍 Join us at our next meetup and be part of a movement where community and innovation meet.
👉 Find community here or at: https://analytics.club
