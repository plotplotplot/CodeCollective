About us

Interested in learning Unity3D, helping others learn Unity3D, Unity3D best practices and/or game development? This group helps members become better Unity3D users and game developers through face-to-face meetings, round tables and lectures. This isn't a group for just programmers, artists are welcome too.
We are dedicated to providing a safe space for all of our events. Our code of conduct can be read here:
**https://tinyurl.com/yykqtzpe**
