11:00 PM
Youth Robotics x Hawaii Tech Week

**We are excited to announce that this event will be running during Hawaii Tech Week .** Please see https://hawaiitechweek.com and sign up. **Mahalo!**

\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-\-

Did you know that Hawai'i has a rich youth robotics scene?

When **Hawaiʻi’s robotics teams compete at the world championships**, they’re not just building robots—they’re building the skills our islands need. Join us for an afternoon **celebrating their achievements** and **connecting students, job seekers, career-changers, and local companies** investing in Hawaiʻi’s tech future. With robot demos!

\-\-\-

Our showcase features local robotics startups, teams from [Island Robotics ](https://www.friendsofislandrobotics.org/) and coaches like **Jared Haiola**, who has guided dozens of Hale'iwa students to VEX IQ World Championships over the past decade. His students don't just learn robotics—they learn to solve problems with limited resources, collaborate under pressure, and build things that work.

**Free Admission** *For Families & Individuals*
Bring your kids. Let them see what's possible when Hawai'i's youth get the right tools and mentorship. Let them talk to the teams. Let them imagine themselves building the technology our islands need.
