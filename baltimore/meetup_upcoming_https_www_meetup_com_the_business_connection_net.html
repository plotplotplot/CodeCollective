About us

This Meetup group is for anyone who would like to make connections with businesses. Either if it’s business to business, business to customer, or business to potential employee. This is a great place to promote your business, find support, and make connections. There will be speakers to educate different types of businesses, networking opportunities, and guidance.  This is a friendly environment where we meet online to help each other grow as professionals.
