About us

If you feel politically stuck in the middle, but want to talk to people, even if they don't politically align with you, this is the group to help bridge the divide in our country. Inspired by, but not affiliated with Michael Smerconish’s The Mingle Project, this group will meet to discuss hot topics and news events with people from different backgrounds and points of view.

If you’re an independent thinker and want to join a conversation with people of diverse views, then you’ve found the right place.
