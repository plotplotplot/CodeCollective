About us

Black + Techie = Us. We're hyped to host Baltimore's tech community! Head to bmoreblack.tech to join our Slack page and stay up-to-date on all our upcoming events

Some of our most popular events are: project nights, speaker nights and social nights. See the individual events for more details.

We are focused on encouraging and improving representation in tech. Therefore this is an inclusive space. All are welcome.  Please respect our goals and mission, and have fun!
