About us

A once‑a‑month gathering for AI practitioners, forward‑thinking investors, executives, and financially curious minds in Baltimore. A place where intelligent capital meets to share insights, compare notes, and build relationships. Once a month we explore emerging tech markets, moderate a capital round table discussion of how to profit from a tech market, and facilitate participant networking. A friendly environment for people who enjoy thinking about markets, capital and the future.

    * AI enthusiasts

    * Anyone with financial curiosity who enjoys exploring markets, profit strategy, risk analysis and high tech
