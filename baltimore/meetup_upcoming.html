What we’re about

This Meetup is organized by Mount Everest Consulting

Through this Meetup, we aim to build a strong community that fosters networking and collaboration among individuals, government and non-government organizations, educational institutions, and industry professionals. Our focus areas include industry trends, AI governance best practices, open-source tools, Capture the Flag (CTF) competitions, and more.

As we know, AI is becoming increasingly integrated into every aspect of life, enhancing productivity while also introducing new risks. Adversaries are leveraging AI tools to refine and strengthen their attack strategies. To address these challenges, each of our Meetups features expert speakers, timely AI industry updates, and educational sessions.

Our mission is to provide valuable knowledge, emerging tools, and actionable insights that empower individuals, families, and organizations to strengthen information security and reduce personal and organizational risk in an AI-driven world.
